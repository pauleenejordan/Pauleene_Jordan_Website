My name is Pauleene Jordan.

La Union Philippines

I grew up in a small town in the Philippines called Tarlac, which is a vibrant and diverse city located in the center of the country. Most people there speaks two dialects. This is where I learned how to speak my native language Tagalog and my other dialect Kapampangan.



Meredith New Hampshire

Then, when I was 17, my family decided to migrate in the US. I was in 11th grade during this time. We lived in a peaceful rural town in New Hampshire called Meredith for 6 months. The town is very different from the farm-type of city I grew up in. Meredith looks like it came straight out of painting during fall season and has so many fun activities in the Winter once the lake turns into ice.



Lawrence Township New Jersey

After few months, we moved to a new state, New Jersey. I love New Jersey. In some ways, the state has resemblance to my old country. There are some Filipino restaurants that serves authentic dishes that reminds me of my grandma's cooking. I started my 12th grade in a new school in New Jersey, where I decided to take AP Computer Science.
